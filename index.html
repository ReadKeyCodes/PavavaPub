Introduction

Parava Publications is a startup publishing agency founded with the motto “the voice of unheard voices.” We exist to give young, promising student authors an opportunity to step into the world of physical publishing—an industry that often feels inaccessible to newcomers. Our mission is to identify potential, nurture creativity, and transform manuscripts into professionally produced books that can stand confidently beside traditionally published works.


We believe that publishing should not be limited to established names or institutions. Parava Publications is built for writers who have something meaningful to say but lack the platform, resources, or guidance to bring their work into print.

About Us

Parava Publications was founded by a group of students who recognized a major gap in the publishing ecosystem. Talented young writers often struggle to find publishers willing to take a chance on new voices, especially when those voices come from student communities. Instead of waiting for validation, the founders chose to create their own publishing platform—one driven by learning, collaboration, and transparency.


From its inception, Parava Publications has functioned as both a creative and organizational learning space. Each founding member contributes a specialized skill, allowing the publication to operate like a professional agency while remaining flexible and student-led. Although still in its early stages, Parava aims to grow into a recognized startup publishing agency that consistently supports student authors and brings their work into physical form.

How to Publish?

Parava Publications offers a clear and supportive publishing process designed specifically for new and student authors. We understand that publishing can be intimidating, especially for first-time writers. Our approach emphasizes guidance, honesty, and collaboration. Every project begins with discussion and mutual understanding, ensuring that both the author’s vision and the publication’s standards are respected.

Self-Publish Through Us

For authors who wish to retain full creative and intellectual control over their work, Parava Publications provides a self-publishing pathway. In this model, authors publish independently while receiving structured support from Parava. This includes editorial assistance, layout and formatting, cover design, printing coordination, and guidance on distribution.


The author remains the sole owner of the work, while Parava acts as a professional facilitator, ensuring the book meets physical publishing standards without compromising the author’s identity.

We Publish for You

Parava Publications also offers a full publishing option for authors who prefer a more guided experience. In this model, Parava takes responsibility for editing, visual identity, layout, printing decisions, and marketing strategy. This option is designed for student authors who may lack the resources or experience to manage publishing independently.


Even in this structure, authors remain informed and involved. Parava prioritizes ethical publishing practices and long-term author growth over short-term profit.

How to Buy Our Books?

Books published by Parava Publications are sold primarily through direct and community-based channels. This allows us to keep prices accessible while maintaining sustainability. Readers can purchase books through pre-orders, direct communication with Parava representatives, or authorized sellers associated with the publication.


This approach strengthens the connection between readers, authors, and the publication, ensuring that each sale directly supports young writers and the growth of Parava Publications.
